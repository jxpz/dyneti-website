Get Started

Integrate DyScan into your mobile checkout flow to prevent:

Account Takeovers

When a bad actor gains access to a good user's account.

Payments Fraud

When a bad actor obtains a list of stolen credit card numbers from the dark web.

How it works

When you integrate DyScan into your mobile checkout flow, your customer can scan her card to enter payment info more easily while DyScan verifies the card is real and present. As a result, more good users transact, while bad actors are blocked
